Our Mission

Many people with solar panels generate extra green energy, but cannot use it all themselves and face limitations like a congested electricity grid, which leads to the customer paying when giving back.

Our product solves this by letting you store heat and use all your self-generated solar power, boosting your return on investment and lowering battery costs.
