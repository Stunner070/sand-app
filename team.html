Our Backgrounds

We are a group of 5 students with diverse backgrounds. We all follow the minor Tech-Based Entrepreneurship at TU Delft in The Netherlands. We met each other during this minor and decided to work together.

Our backgrounds are:

  • Computer Science (Sofyan)
  • Mechanical Engineering (Joey)
  • Electrical Engineering (Rutger)
  • Business Administration (Bouwe)
  • Maritime Engineering (Jan)

Our journey began when we recognized that many homeowners with solar panels faced a critical problem: they generate excess green energy but cannot use it all themselves. Grid congestion and unfavorable feed-in rates meant their investment wasn't reaching its full potential. Together, we're committed to making sustainable energy storage accessible, efficient, and economically viable for everyone.
